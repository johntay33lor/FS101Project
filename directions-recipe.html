How to Make Mac and Cheeese



Directions

For Ingredients, follow the link.
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour and cook 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  3. Stir in milk, salt, and pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, until it boils and thickens.
  4. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add and cook Macaroni until tender. Drain
  5. Add Cheddar and American cheeses to milk mixture. Stir until cheese melts.
  6. Combine cheese sauce and macaroni in baking dish. Mix well.
  7. Bake in preheated oven until hot and bubbly. Let cool 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
